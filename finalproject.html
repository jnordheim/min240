Home

Welcome to Skate Appreciate! Explore the website by selecting on the tabs.

Find Skateparks: The maps feature of the app allows skaters to find parks in their vicinity. Similar to a GPS, it will tell you what parks are buzzing and which ones are vacant. Depending on the person, they can choose to go to a park of their choosing.

Link Up: Finding people to skate with is harder than it seems. Not having a skate partner is like playing tennis by yourself; nobody is there to push you. That's why having the linking up feature is essential for getting better as a skater.

Upload Tricks: At the end of the day, the goal of a skater is to record your tricks and upload them (or get sponsored, if you're trying to go pro). The tricks timeline of the app allows skaters watch their peers' tricks and to commend them for their hardwork. And of course show off their own tricks!
